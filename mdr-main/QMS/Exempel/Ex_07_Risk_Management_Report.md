## 1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
This report summarizes the risk management activities for **SkinScan AI v1.0**. All activities defined in the Risk Management Plan (RMP-001) have been executed. Based on the analysis, all identified risks have been mitigated to an acceptable level, and the clinical benefits of the device outweigh the residual risks.

## 2. SCOPE OF ANALYSIS
The analysis covered the entire software lifecycle, including:
- **Algorithms:** AI-driven image analysis for skin lesions.
- **Cybersecurity:** Data transmission, cloud storage, and patient privacy (GDPR).
- **Usability:** User interface interactions and potential for use errors.
- **Interoperability:** Connectivity with standard smartphone camera hardware.

## 3. RISK MANAGEMENT SUMMARY
A total of **28 hazards** were identified and evaluated.
- **Total risks identified:** 28
- **Risks reduced to "Low" (Acceptable):** 26
- **Risks requiring Benefit-Risk justification:** 2 (False Negatives and Cybersecurity-related data breaches).

## 4. BENEFIT-RISK ANALYSIS (MDR Requirement)
Despite the implementation of state-of-the-art risk controls, a residual risk of a **False Negative** result remains (as is inherent in all diagnostic AI algorithms). 

**Clinical Benefit:** SkinScan AI provides high-speed, accessible screening for early-stage melanoma. Early detection significantly increases survival rates and reduces the burden on healthcare systems.
**Conclusion:** The clinical benefit of early detection for a large population outweighs the remote risk of a false negative result, provided the user is instructed to consult a physician regardless of the result if symptoms persist.

## 5. POST-MARKET SURVEILLANCE (PMS) PLAN
The Risk Management File will be updated continuously based on data from:
- **Incident reporting:** Any reported clinical misdiagnosis.
- **Cybersecurity monitoring:** New vulnerabilities in cloud infrastructure.
- **Usage data:** Error rates in image uploads or low-light warnings.

## 6. FINAL STATEMENT
The risk management process has been completed in accordance with **ISO 14971:2019**. The device is considered safe for its intended use, and its safety profile is consistent with the generally acknowledged state of the art.
